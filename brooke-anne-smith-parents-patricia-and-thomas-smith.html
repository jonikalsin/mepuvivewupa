Meet Brooke Anne Smith Parents: Patricia and Thomas Smith!

 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  879 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Get to know Patricia and Thomas Smith, the parents of actress Brooke Anne Smith. The actress has recently been announced to be a cast member in General Hospital.

Read all we know about her folks via this article below.

Who Are Brooke Anne Smith Parents?

Brooke Anne Smith is the daughter of Patricia “Patty” Smith and Thomas Smith. The actress is very close to her family members and features them all the time on her socials.

The American actress is best known for her lead series regular role in two seasons of Tyler Perry’s “Too Close To Home” where she played a drug-addicted sexual assault survivor appearing in a total of 16 episodes. She is also known for her recurring role on MTV’s hit show “Awkward.”

Brooke landed a lead series regular role in Oscar winner Renee Zellweger’s six-million dollar pilot “Cinnamon Girl” which was directed by critically acclaimed Gavin O’Connor. She made her motion picture debut as a lead in Walt Disney’s “Max Keeble’s Big Move” and since then, has gone on to several television shows and feature films to date.

In 2023, Brooke is taking on the role of Molly at General Hospital. Brooke took over the role of Holiday Mia Kriegel in the July 19 episode.

Brooke recently took to her IG and wrote, “Thank you to everyone who has been so kind to welcome me onto @generalhospitalabc I am honored to be a part of such an iconic show😱🫣🥰 To all my friends here; I have been quiet on social media as I did not know how to answer why I dyed my hair. 🤣 Now the cat is out of the bag and I can share all the news! Big thanks to @generalhospitalabc  #frankvalentini #markteschner #lisabooth + the whole cast and crew for welcoming the new kid. 💕.”

Brooke has previously appeared as Lori Price in the Charles Manson thriller Aquarius on NBC, Maria Diorio in the CSI: Crime Scene Investigation episode “Unleashed,” and Claire Tate in the Cold Case episode “Family.”

Born in 1984, Brooke is 39 years of age.

Meet Patricia Smith, Brooke Anne Smith Mother

Patricia “Patty” Hughes Smith is the mother of actress Brooke Anne Smith.

On Mother’s Day 2022, Brooke took to her IG and wished her mother, “Happy Mother’s Day to the best mom a girl could ask for! I love all of our adventures and I cannot wait to have so many more with you! Love you more than words can say! ❤️❤️❤️❤️ @pattyhughessmith.”

Patricia celebrates her birthday on 27 May. In 2019, Brooke posted an old picture of her mother and wished her, “Happy Birthday to my gorgeous mom @pattyhughessmith !! I love you so so so so so much!!! ❤️❤️❤️🎉🎉❤️.”

Based on the post, Patricia was once employed as an air hostess.

Furthermore, Patty wished her daughter on her birthday in March 2023. She wished her daughter, “Happy Birthday to my precious only daughter, Brooke Anne Smith 🍾🥂🎁🕺💃🎂🎉❣️.”

Per Patty’s Facebook, she attended Valdosta State University and Albany High School. She loves “travel tennis golf sand sea sunsets…Glam sparkle grub football front porches and sweet tea.” Although she is based in California, she is a native of Albany, Georgia.

Meet Thomas Smith, Brooke Anne Smith Father

Brooke Anne Smith’s father is named Thomas Smith. He worked as an airline captain but his career details are not available currently.

On Father’s Day Post in 2012, Brooke wished her father, “Happy Father’s Day!! <3 My dad!!!.” Likewise, on Mother’s Day 2013, the actress wished her mom, “I have the best mama on the planet!!! Mom, you are beautiful inside and out and I am so lucky to be your daughter. Love you more than anything. ❤❤❤.”

In 2023, Thomas reached 73 years old.
