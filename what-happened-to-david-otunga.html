What happened to David Otunga?

 Posted on June 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  552 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

David Daniel Otunga, an American actor, lawyer, and former professional wrestler, has had a diverse career in the entertainment industry.

From his early days in WWE to his transition into legal advisory roles and non-wrestling positions, Otunga's journey has been marked by various milestones and changes.

Together with Fan Arch, we'll delve into the details of what happened to David Otunga, exploring his professional wrestling career, his foray into legal advisory and commentary roles, and his non-wrestling endeavors.

Professional Wrestling Career

David Otunga's professional wrestling career was marked by significant achievements and memorable moments. He signed a developmental contract with WWE in 2008 and worked in FCW before moving to NXT.

Notably, Otunga was the runner-up on the first season of NXT. He then became an original member of The Nexus, a faction that left a lasting impact on the WWE universe.

As part of The Nexus, Otunga became a two-time WWE Tag Team Champion, solidifying his presence in the wrestling world.

The Nexus (2010-2011)

During his time with The Nexus, Otunga was part of several high-profile storylines and matches, including a notable feud with John Cena.

The faction's clash with established superstars and their involvement in championship pursuits added depth to Otunga's wrestling career.

After The Nexus, Otunga's trajectory in wrestling took various turns, leading to his eventual retirement from in-ring competition in 2015.

Legal Advisory and Commentary Roles

Following his retirement from wrestling, Otunga ventured into legal advisory and commentary roles within WWE.

He became an on-screen character, offering his legal services to fellow wrestlers and participating in storylines involving general managers and on-screen personalities.

He appeared on RAW, SmackDown, and Main Event as a commentator and panelist, contributing to the overall presentation of WWE programming.

Non-Wrestling Roles (2015–2018)

From 2015 to 2018, Otunga's presence expanded beyond wrestling as he took on non-wrestling roles within WWE.

His involvement in the Raw Pre-Show and commentary team demonstrated his adaptability and commitment to evolving within the industry.

Otunga's contributions to WWE's broadcasting and analysis added a fresh perspective to the presentation of WWE events, solidifying his position as a multifaceted talent.

Beyond Wrestling: Acting and Legal Career

Apart from his wrestling and broadcasting endeavors, David Otunga has pursued acting and legal ventures. His appearances in various films, including "The Call" and "What Happen Last Night," showcased his acting abilities.

Also, Otunga's legal background, which includes a degree from Harvard Law School and work at a prestigious law firm, exemplifies his intellectual acumen and professional versatility.

These non-wrestling pursuits have further enriched Otunga's professional portfolio, highlighting his multidimensional talents and interests.

Personal Life and Transition

David Otunga's personal life has also been a subject of public interest, particularly his relationship with singer and actress Jennifer Hudson.

The couple's union and subsequent separation have been topics of public discussion, shedding light on the personal challenges and transitions experienced by Otunga outside the realm of professional wrestling. 

 

David Otunga's journey from professional wrestling to legal advisory, commentary, acting, and personal endeavors is a testament to his versatility and determination. His contributions to WWE, coupled with his ventures beyond wrestling, have showcased his ability to excel in diverse domains.

While his wrestling career marked a significant chapter in his professional life, Otunga's evolution into multifaceted roles underscores his dynamic presence in the entertainment industry.
