Hannah is a makeup, beauty, and fashion YouTube star who posted routine, collection, haul, DIY, and tutorial videos. Her first YouTube post came in April of 2014 and was titled “Get Ready With Me – Simple Neutral Smokey Eye & Nude Lip”.

Her love for makeup & fashion began to blossom around the age of sixteen when she first discovered the world of YouTube herself.

Some months after turning eighteen, she began working for Mac as a retail artist for a while. It has probably been one of her biggest achievements as a self-taught makeup artist.
