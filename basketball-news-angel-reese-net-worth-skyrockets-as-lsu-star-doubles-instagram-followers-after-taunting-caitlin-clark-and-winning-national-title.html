Meanwhile, Johnson’s electric style of play has made her a fan favorite, and her engaging personality on social media has only added to her appeal.

The Final Four weekend has not only boosted the social media followings of Reese and Johnson, but also that of Iowa’s Caitlin Clark. Her Instagram followers more than doubled from 230,000 to 537,000, thanks to her exceptional performance on the court, which has elevated her to a rising star status in women’s basketball.
