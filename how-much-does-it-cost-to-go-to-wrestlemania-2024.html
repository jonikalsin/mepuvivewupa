How much does it cost to go to Wrestlemania 2024?

 Posted on June 18, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  555 words  |  Martina Birk

WrestleMania, the "Super Bowl of pro wrestling," is a highly anticipated annual event that draws fans from all over the world. The cost of attending WrestleMania 2024 varies significantly, and in this comprehensive article, we will explore the various aspects of the expenses associated with attending WrestleMania 2024, including ticket prices, additional costs, and related events.

Ticket Prices for WWE WrestleMania 40

The ticket prices for WWE WrestleMania 40 cover a wide range, catering to different preferences and budgets. As of the available information, here are the price ranges for WrestleMania 40 tickets:

  • Most Expensive Tickets: Two-day passes are available for $23,625, night one tickets for $16,538, and night two tickets for $14,057. Tailgate tickets are also available for $234.
  • Cheapest Tickets: Fans can purchase two-day passes for $630, night one tickets for $217, and night two tickets for $196. Tailgate tickets are also available for $234.

Comparing these prices with the previous year's event, the two-day pass for WrestleMania 39 started at $227, with the highest ticket price around $14,000. The first night of WrestleMania 39 started at $67, with the highest ticket price around $16,000, and the second night started at $84, with the highest ticket price around $16,000. These comparisons demonstrate the significant variation in ticket prices over the years.

Factors Affecting Ticket Prices

Several factors contribute to the fluctuation in WrestleMania ticket prices, including the venue, the historical significance of the event, and the presence of popular wrestling superstars. It is essential to note that as the event date approaches, ticket prices may fluctuate, and fans should consider this aspect when planning their attendance.

Availability of WrestleMania 40 Tickets

Despite the varying ticket prices, it is noteworthy that there are still plenty of tickets available for WrestleMania 40. The event is set to take place at the Lincoln Financial Field for two nights, and the venue can accommodate up to 67,594 people. While some seats may be affected by the event set, the attendance is expected to exceed 60,000, with over 90,000 tickets sold on the first day of ticket sales, breaking the all-time gate record set by WrestleMania 39.

Additional Costs and Considerations

In addition to the ticket prices, attendees should also consider other expenses associated with attending WrestleMania 40, including travel, accommodation, and merchandise. It's important to factor in these additional costs when planning to attend the event.

WWE World of WrestleMania 40

Aside from the main event, WWE is also hosting the World of WrestleMania, which offers various immersive experiences for fans. The ticket prices for the World of WrestleMania range from $30 to $240 for adults, with additional options for kids between the ages of 2 and 12. The event will feature a central main stage hosting roundtable discussions with top WWE Superstars, a WWE 2K24 gaming tournament, and live podcast recordings. There will also be VIP Experiences available for purchase at a later date.

The cost of attending WrestleMania 2024 can vary significantly based on factors such as ticket prices, additional expenses, and related events. The availability of tickets, the historical significance of the event, and the immersive experiences offered by WWE contribute to the overall expenses associated with attending WrestleMania 40. As fans eagerly await the grand event, it is essential to carefully consider all costs and factors to ensure an enjoyable and memorable WrestleMania experience.
