MONICA, ERICA CAMPBELL AND DAUGHTERS SHINE AT ESSENCE FEST 2023

 Posted on July 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  317 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Erica Campbell and Monica, accompanied by their daughters Krista Campbell and Laiyah Brown, made a memorable appearance at the 2023 ESSENCE Festival of Culture™ in New Orleans, Louisiana. Spanning from June 29 to July 3, 2023, the Essence Fest of 2023 is a vibrant celebration that brings together people of different backgrounds.

During the festival’s second day, Monica took the stage and delivered an incredible performance. Monica captivated the audience with hit songs like “Don’t Take It Personal (Just One of Dem Days),” “Angel of Mine,” and “So Gone,” among others. She concluded her set by paying homage to the vibrant city of New Orleans, all while her daughter Laiyah watched in awe.

“I can always depend on my Mama,” Laiyah shared in the caption of photos featuring her and Monica at the event.

Meanwhile, Erica Campbell graced the stage at The Get Lifted Stage, which has become a revered Sunday tradition at the festival, fostering spiritual communion through song, dance, and fellowship.  During her performance, Erica called upon her daughter Krista, who was singing background, to join her in front of the stage. Krista moved the crowd with a heartfelt rendition of “Oh, how I love Jesus,” showcasing her undeniable talent.

Sharing the experience on Instagram,she wrote, “Oh how I love Jesus….my first time singing at #essencefest sharing the stage w/ my momma @imericacampbell.

“I’m soooo proud! Your first performance was great!!!” Erica responded, showering her daughter with love and pride.

The 29th annual ESSENCE Festival of Culture brought together the best of the industry and attracted numerous celebrities, from well-known figures to rising talents. With its diverse offerings, the festival served as a platform to connect with the people of New Orleans, highlighting the city’s rich cultural heritage.

Attendees were treated to a weekend of entertainment, while also having the opportunity to explore various aspects of life, including business, health and wellness, and culinary delights.
