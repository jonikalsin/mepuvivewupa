Contestants of The Voice: la plus belle voix

As mentioned above Elsa is one of the eliminated contestants of the France version of the TF1's singing competition show The Voice (season 6). The judges of the show were Florent Pagny, Mika, Zazie, and Matt Pokora.

During the blind auditions 6, Roses sing a song 'Somewhere Only We Know' by the English rock band, Keane. After the performance, coaches Florence, Mika, and Matt, press their "JE VOUS VEUX" button, and Roses chooses to be in the judge Zazie's team. 

Elsa was doing well. Meanwhile, in the Battle 3 round, she loses the competition. Her last performance was the song Je t'aimais, Je t'aime et Je t'aimerai (I loved you, I love you and I will love you), by the French singer Francis Cabrel. Additionally, the winner of season 6 of The Voice (France version) was Lisandro Cuxi, Matt Pokora's team member.
