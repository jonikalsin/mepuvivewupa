What Happened To Elise Ivy On QVC, Is She Leaving, Where Is She Going?

 Posted on May 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  483 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Elise Ivy is an American journalist, leaving QVC for a new chapter in her life. Stay tuned to know the reason behind it.

Elise was working in QVC as a program host and is leaving the channel. She had been with the channel for about six and a half years.

Ivy made a successful career as a host and a presenter. She is also in the retail, beauty, fashion, accessories, health, and wellness industry. Ivy is also a personal trainer and a Pilates teacher.

The journalist also worked as a co-host in the QVC's Basic Beauty podcast.

Elise had a knee interest in beauty and fashion since childhood. So, it was a perfect job for her. She also had an acting career and had appeared in the television series Terminator: The Sarah Connor, the movie: Please Give, and the comedy-drama: Take Care.

In 2006, she worked as a sales and service representative for Varsity Brands. She continued working for Varsity Brands till 2011. Between 2011 and 2013, she worked as a World Cruise Hostess.

Elise Ivy is leaving QVC: What Happened to Her on QVC?

After working for more than six and a half years in QVC as a program host she has announced her departure from the channel.

She posted about leaving the channel on her Instagram. The journalist asked the fans to keep on supporting her in her new journey.

The host left the channel on December 30, 2021. The exact reason behind leaving QVC has not been revealed.

Elise's full name is Elise Michelle Ivy. To get the job in QVC, she had given two auditions in three years. In 2013, she gave her first audition but did not get selected. In 2015 she gave another audition and this time got selected.

Elise had attended the University of Nebraska in Lincoln with a cheer scholarship. In the University, she was a university champion weightlifter.

Elise Ivy Is Going Somewhere: Where Is She Going?

Elise Ivy is moving on to a new chapter in her life.

She has started her lifestyle blog on the internet. She has written about her meandering path to find true love in the blog.

In this blog, she has spilled the beans on her real-life romance. She has written everything about her life and love story in this blog chapter by chapter.

The journalist has described the blog as the stories from the heart. She has also provided the link to her blog in her Instagram handle @elsie.ivy.

Currently, she is busy writing blogs on her life and real-life love story.

The blogger and writer, is currently living in Los Angeles, California with her parents and other family members.
