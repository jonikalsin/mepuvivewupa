Early Life and Childhood

Maddy Spidell came to this beautiful world on March 31, 2000, in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, the United States. Currently, she is 23 years old and her birth sign is Aries. Further, her mother’s name is Mary Spidell and her father’s name is Michael Spidell. Her mother works in the Milwaukee Public School system. She has two siblings, an older brother, Thad Spidell, and a younger brother, Jedd Spidell. Jedd is a development coordinator at the Muscular Dystrophy Association and Thad is at the U.S. Air Force.

Concerning her education, she graduated from Greendale High School in Wisconsin and has worked for some local businesses. Then, she attended the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee and studied business, and is set to graduate in 2022.

Career and Professional Life

Maddy is a dancer and started her career at the age of two at a local dance studio and went on the train to the Young Dance Academy. Further, she is also trained in Jazz, Lyrical, Tap, and Ballet.

During her High school days, she performed with the High School Cheerleading Squad. In the first year, she was the captain of the Greendale High Poms squad and in the second year, she was a Squad member and also won several awards along with her squad including the title of Miss Badgerette, in her junior year. In 2012, she began to post ballet photos.

Additionally, she performed all over the country, and in 2016 she performed at the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade which was held in New York City. She has also performed in competitions all over the country. She managed all this while working in stores and also as a dance instructor. Moreover, she was photographed at a 2019 KISS concert.

Apart from that, she started posting her work through social media and quickly became an influencer and model on Instagram. She often posted dance videos and shared fashion ideas alongside her cute dog.
