Rylan Clark displays his 'swollen' face (but keeps his new teeth under wraps!)

 Posted on July 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  547 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Rylan Clark-Neal displayed his swollen face in a new Instagram video on Wednesday – after getting his veneers replaced. 

The Big Brother star, 33, touched his cheeks and sported messy locks in the video, captioned ‘swollen’.

The star, who hasn’t yet revealed his teeth, was clad in a dressing gown as he continues to recover from the procedure.

Recovery: Rylan Clark-Neal displayed his swollen face in a new Instagram video on Wednesday – after getting his veneers replaced

 Rylan first revealed on Monday that he will be getting new teeth eight years after having his veneers fitted, joking that ‘sometimes you just have to let go.’

Updating his fans on Twitter, the presenter wrote: ‘After 8 years. It hurts, so much, but sometimes you just have to let go. I’ve taken the decision to say goodbye… to my teeth [laughing emoji] new smile imminent [smiling emoji]’. 

Journalist Dan Wootton replied: ‘BOMBSHELL NEWS!’ while a loyal fan penned ‘Aww Bye bye Rylan’s teeth! thanks for All the smiles! xxx’.

The It Takes Two host also shared the news on Instagram, tagging cosmetic dentist Dr Richard Marques in the post. 

TOWIE star Georgia Kousoulou commented laughing faces, while Vicky Pattison wrote: ‘Hahahahah can’t wait’.

Back in 2013, the Essex-born star showed off his shiny set of veneers after making his debut hosting Big Brother spin-off show Big Brother’s Bit On The Side.

Viewers were quick to comment on Rylan’s blindingly white smile, but the TV personality took the jibes well at the time, later writing: ‘Newsflash! My teeth will be fronting new BB show “Bit On The Big Side” coming soon….. #onlyjoking.’

The new teeth announcement comes after the X Factor star revealed his bank account was targeted by hackers.
