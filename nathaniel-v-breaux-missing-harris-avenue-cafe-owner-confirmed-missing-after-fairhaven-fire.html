Nathaniel V. Breaux Missing – A devastating early-morning fire in Fairhaven has left multiple businesses in ruins, with authorities struggling to locate one person associated with the affected building. The owner of Harris Avenue Cafe, Nathaniel V. Breaux, 39, has been reported missing to the Bellingham Police, adding an additional layer of concern and sadness to the already tragic incident. The fire broke out just before midnight, prompting a swift response from several fire engines.

The building, which housed the former Tony’s Coffee and the current Harris Avenue Cafe and Old Independent Coffee House, now stands as a charred shell, marking the end of an era for these local businesses. The private offices above the two locations were also consumed by the destructive flames. The confirmation of Nathaniel V. Breaux’s missing status intensifies the gravity of the situation, as friends, family, and the community at large anxiously await updates on his whereabouts. The sense of loss extends beyond the physical damage to the building, encompassing the potential loss of a beloved member of the local business community.
