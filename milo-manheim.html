Milo Manheim

Milo Manheim is an American actor known for his role as Zed in the Disney Channel Original Movie Zombies. He made his on-screen debut in 2009.

Milo’s Bio & Family

Milo Manheim was born on March 6, 2001, in Los Angeles, California, United States. He is the only son of Camryn Manheim and Jeffrey Brezovar. His mother Camryn is the Emmy and Golden Globe award-winning actress.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BqX9XFbHC-x/

Milo graduated from Crossroads School for Arts & Sciences in Santa Monica, California in 2019. He is currently studying acting at New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts.

Career & Net Worth

Milo started acting at the age of six in a local after-school program in Culver City. Later, he starred in 20 different musicals with Liza Monjauze Productions in 2008. He made his on-screen debut in the guest role on the CBS television series Ghost Whisperer in 2009.

In 2018, Manheim was cast in a lead role in the Disney Channel television movie, Zombie. He reprised his role as Zed in the film, Zombies 2, which was premiered on Valentine’s Day, 2020.

Apart from acting, he is also a talented dancer, who competed on the 27th season of the reality show, Dancing with the Stars. He became the runner-up on the show along with dancing partner Witney Carson.

His Personal Life

The 19-year-old was in a relationship with an Instagram star Holiday Mia Kriegel. The couple dated for several months until their breakup in late 2018.

Later, Milo was rumored to be dating Instagram model  Alexis Ren as they were spotted holding hands at the Coachella music festival in April 2019. He is reportedly single at present.
